Bringing the Best Arcade Games to Life

At Game Wave Crib, we believe in delivering top-notch gaming experiences to our outlets across Lagos. We invite you to be a part of this exciting venture by choosing games from our collection to install at any of our outlets. With a seamless revenue-sharing model, you’ll enjoy consistent returns on your investment without worrying about the hassle of operations.

How It Works

  1. Choose Your Games
    - Browse our wide range of arcade games on our website. With prices ranging from ₦2,500 – ₦6,000 per play session, there’s a game for everyone, from timeless classics to modern favorites.
  2. We Handle the Setup
    - Once you select your game, we’ll install it in one of our vibrant Game Wave Crib outlets. We manage everything—from placement to technical setup—to ensure the best gaming experience for our customers.
  3. Profit Sharing
    - Sit back and watch the profits roll in! With our 60/40 revenue-sharing model, you receive 60% of the revenue generated from your game, while Game Wave Crib retains 40% to cover running costs, maintenance, and customer support.


Why Choose Game Wave Crib?

  1. Affordable Investment
    With low upfront costs, you can start earning quickly.
  2. Hassle-Free Management
    We handle operations, technical support, and maintenance, ensuring your games are always running perfectly.
  3. Prime Outlets
    Your games are placed in our well-located and highly trafficked outlets, attracting a steady stream of players.
  4. Transparent Reporting
    Get regular updates on the performance and revenue of your games.
